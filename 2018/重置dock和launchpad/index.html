重置 Dock 和 Launchpad

Written by Jepp Hu with ♥ on January 6, 2018 in Mac

用来解决 APP 图标异常的问题，APP 图标会恢复成默认排序

defaults write com.apple.dock ResetLaunchPad -bool true; killall Dock
