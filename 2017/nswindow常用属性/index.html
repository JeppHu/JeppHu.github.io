NSWindow 常用属性

Written by Jepp Hu with ♥ on March 28, 2017 in Cocoa

隐藏标题栏

[self setTitleVisibility:NSWindowTitleHidden];

window StyleMask 设置为 NSFullSizeContentViewWindowMask 时，标题栏不绘制背景

[self setTitlebarAppearsTransparent:YES];

通过拖动背景来移动整个 window

[self setMovableByWindowBackground:YES];

完全透明的 window

[self setBackgroundColor:[NSColor clearColor]];
[self setOpaque:NO];
